About Me

Asfia Bint Abdullah holds a master's degree in Islamic Studies and is a passionate advocate for Islamic History and heritage. She is the author of the bestselling book Salahuddin Ayyubi: The Story of a Warrior, avaliable on Amazon. Known for her ability to weave historical accuracy with spiritual wisdom and engaging storytelling, she continues to inspire young readers. Her commitment to exploring sacred sites such as Masjid al-Aqsa reflects on her dedication to connecting readers with their faith and the richness of Islamic Culture.
